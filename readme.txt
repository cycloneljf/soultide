Feature: Energy and Level System
In this mod, there is an energy and level system for both the player character and equipment. 
Exclusive weapons and gear have energy, which can be increased by adding related items. When the energy reaches its limit (50 points multiplied by the level), the player can level up. Upon reaching certain levels, the player can unlock new abilities or enhance existing ones.
There are five different attributes: Gem, Shadow, Moon, Lost, and Flesh. 
The current gear can have a maximum of two attributes out of these five.
At levels 2, 5, and 9, the player needs to use corresponding breakthrough cores to unlock new abilities.

Feature: Quailty System
Some items in the mod automatically advance to the next Quailty once the corresponding level is reached.
 This advancement affects the attributes of the items. The tiers are represented by colors such as green, blue, purple, gold, and red.

Feature: SP System
The player characters in the mod have a fourth attribute called SP, which is displayed along with the three-dimensional attributes.
Having high SP provides damage bonus, defense reduction, and increased movement speed, while low SP has the opposite effect. 
Currently, there are no active skills that consume SP.(this will update soon)

Individual Weapon: Scythe of the Desolation Garden
Base attack: 36, Attack range: 2.4. Level cap: 10 (configurable).
Unlimited durability, emits light.
Upgrading the weapon increases the base attack and dimensional attack. At level 8, the base attack becomes 36 + 78, and the dimensional attack becomes 66. Range attack is unlocked after level 3, and it improves with each level. 
The following traits can be enhanced as the level increases: Cold Fear, Life Drain, Assassination (chance to double loot when killing), Harvest (additional loot).

Individual Helmet: Black Thorn Crown
Defense: 54 - 99%. Comes with a frenzy aura. When equipped by Fleuricia, it has a reverse mental aura effect. It has attributes such as Shadow Level, Heat Insulation, Waterproof, Acceleration, and Sandstorm Resistance.
Individual Amulet: Sea Urchin Brooch

Attributes: Warmth, Waterproof, Acceleration. Unlockable effects at higher levels include Frost Protection, Temperature Stone, Lazyman's Charm (able to pick up multiple items at once), and Construction Charm.

Candies and Candy Bags are also included in the mod.

Crystal Tower is a technology-unlocked building.
